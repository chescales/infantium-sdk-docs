==================
Appendix: Plugins
==================

We are conscious that most of our partners do not program directly in native Android, and thus we have created a set of
plugins that allow direct communication from different frameworks with the Native SDK. Here is the list of Frameworks
currently supported. If you do not find your framework below don't get discouraged! `Contact us`_ and we will be more than
happy to give you assistance to create a new plugin in any framework you might use.
